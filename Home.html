if you are seeking this website to enroll in the school, you should follow these steps:-
  1. You must pass these requirements:
    • Your nationality is egyptian.
    • Your first degree relatives' nationalities are egyptians.

    • Your age do not exceed 18.
  2. Prepare your file with 18 personal pictures.
  3. Send your information to the School Enrollment Office via email.
  4. The enrollment office will send you the link of the acceptance test. You must get 80% or higher to pass.
  5. Enrollment Office will finish the rest of enrollment process digitally.
  6. Wait the response and check your email daily.
